About This Establishment:

This establishment was founded on June 21st of 2025. Our goal is to have everybody's swimming goggles be as clean as can be. To do this we have a filtration pump using the finest of clean waters. We use a premium sponge to clean it off (only premium experience). We have two different experiences of cleaning. Normal experience choosers get their goggles cleaned using our engineered cleaner for goggles to get the outside, inside, and lenses. Premium experience chooser get hand washed using our premium sponge. We deep clean the goggles making sure there is no dirt.
